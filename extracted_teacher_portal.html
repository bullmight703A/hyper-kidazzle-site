Identifying Common Illnesses

Hand, Foot, and Mouth Disease (HFMD)

A common viral illness usually affecting infants and children younger than 5 years old.

What to look for:
  • Fever and sore throat
  • Painful sores in the mouth (herpangina)
  • Skin rash (flat red spots, sometimes with blisters) on palms of hands and soles of feet
