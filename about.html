Our Legacy

Three decades. Three states. One mission:
Delivering elite early childhood education with heart, purpose, and innovation.

Since 1994

From Humble Beginnings

KIDazzle Child Care began more than 31 years ago as a family-run center born from a simple belief: quality child care should be accessible to every working family.

Over the years, KIDazzle expanded across the Southeast with one principle intact: invest deeply in people — children, families, teachers, and communities.

What We Stand For

Love & Nurture

Children learn best when they feel safe, loved, and understood.

Creativity

We foster imagination and encourage authentic exploration through hands-on learning.

Safety

Our centers prioritize secure environments, rigorous protocols, and trusted educators.

Growth with Purpose

Our expansion across Georgia, Tennessee, and Florida reflects a commitment to community impact, not franchising. Every KIDazzle center is unique — rooted in local culture but unified by our mission.
