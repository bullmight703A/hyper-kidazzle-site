Our Legacy

Three decades. Three states. One mission.

Since 1994

From Humble Beginnings

KIDazzle Child Care was founded over 31 years ago with a singular vision: to provide elite early childhood education accessible to working families. We started as a single center, driven by the belief that quality care is a right, not a privilege.

Chef Prepared Nutrition

Unlike many centers that serve processed, pre-packaged food, KIDazzle invests in commercial-grade kitchens and professional chefs at our locations. We believe fresh, hot, balanced meals are essential for learning.
