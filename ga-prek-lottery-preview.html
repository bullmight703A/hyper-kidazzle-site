Requirements Checklist

What You Need to Qualify

The Georgia Lottery Pre-K Program has strict eligibility rules. Ensure your child meets the age requirement before submitting enrollment papers.

Who is Eligible?

Age Criterion: Child must be 4 years old on or before September 1, 2026.

Residency: Must be a legal resident of Georgia (proof of residency required).

Status: Available to all children regardless of income, on a first-come, first-served or lottery basis.

Child must be born between Sept 2, 2021 and Sept 1, 2022 to qualify for the 2026-2027 school year.

Required Documents

Proof of Birth: Certified Birth Certificate or Passport.

Proof of GA Residency: Current electric, gas, water bill or a lease agreement showing current name and address (PO boxes not accepted).

GA Immunization Form 3231: Up-to-date pediatric immunization record.

GA Screenings Form 3300: Complete eye, ear, dental, and nutrition screening certificates.

Bring these documents to the campus after applying online to finalize your spot.

Frequently Asked Questions

How much does the Georgia Lottery Pre-K cost?

It is 100% Free. The program covers all instructional hours (6.5 hours per day). Any additional early-morning drop-off or after-school care is billed separately as an add-on, but the core Pre-K curriculum and tuition are fully funded.

What is the exact age requirement?

To qualify for the 2026-2027 school year, your child must be 4 years old on or before September 1, 2026. There are no exceptions allowed by the state for this date rule.

How are students selected in the lottery?

KIDazzle manages state-funded allocation seats. Applying early ensures your documents are audited and queued for the seat allocation draw or waiting list. Registration fills up extremely quickly in the 30310 area, so applying early is recommended.
