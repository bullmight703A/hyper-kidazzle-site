Our Roots

From a Women's Shelter to a Regional Standard

KIDazzle began 31 years ago with a mission rooted in necessity. We started inside a women's shelter, providing stability and safety for families in transition.

Driven by the belief that elite education is a right, we expanded into urban centers across the Southeast to ensure every child has a bright start.
